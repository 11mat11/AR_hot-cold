## Camera Usage & AR
The **Echoes: Audio AR Hunt**  uses the device's camera solely for the purpose of displaying **augmented reality (AR)** content.  
No camera data is **recorded**, **processed**, or **transmitted** to any servers by the developer.  
All AR visual processing occurs locally on the user's device.

## Google Play Games Services
This application uses **Google Play Games Services** to provide:
* **Leaderboards** (to compare scores with other players).
* **Achievements** (to track game progress).

By signing in to Google Play Games Services within the application, you acknowledge that your game data (such as scores, progress, and achievement history) is transmitted to and stored by Google. This data is handled in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

For more information, please consult the Google Privacy Policy:  
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
